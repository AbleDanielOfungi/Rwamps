About Rwampara farm Institute

Rwampara Farm Institute is located at Igamba Hill, Bungyereza cell, Nyeihanga Parish, Buteraniro-Nyeihanga Town Council, Rwampara County, Rwampara District 23 miles along Mbarara Kabale Highway 300 meters from the main road.

  • MOTTO: With skills we advance
  • VISSION: To produce Knowledgeable, skilled and morally upright citizens
  • MISSION:Providing graduates with relevant Knowledge, skills and competence for production and self reliance.

Why Choose Us?

At Rwampara Farm Institute, we emphasize practical, hands-on experience in agriculture and related fields. Students actively participate in farming activities, learning real-world techniques that they can immediately apply on their farms or in agricultural careers.

Popular Courses

Agriculture

National Certificate in agricultural production

The National Certificate in Agricultural Production (NCAP) is a foundational program designed to equip learners with practical skills, knowledge, and techniques in modern agricultural practices.

Rwampara Farm Institute

Poultry

Poultry Production

The Poultry Production Short Course is a hands-on program designed to equip participants with the skills and knowledge needed to manage a successful poultry farming business. This course covers various aspects of poultry production, including housing, feeding, disease management, and marketing of poultry products.

Rwampara Farm Institute

Banana

Banana Production

The Banana Production Short Course is designed to provide participants with practical knowledge and skills in growing and managing banana plantations for maximum yield and profitability. It covers modern agricultural practices, pest and disease control, post-harvest handling, and market strategies for banana products.
